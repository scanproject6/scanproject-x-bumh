About SCAN Project

SCAN (Smart Canteen Access Network) is a food and beverage ordering system innovation developed by a team Concept of technology students in collaboration with Chemical Engineering Entrepreneurship.

With this system, despite limited space and signal, consumers can still obtain menu and price information quickly without having to gather at the business location, and minimize signal difficulties so as to create time efficiency, can increase customer satisfaction, and a modern image for student MSMEs.

With our digital ordering system, we make it easy for students and staff to enjoy food and drinks without having to wait in long lines.
